# Nostra Group Website - Replit Build Instructions

## Overview
Create a sophisticated, professional investment firm website for Nostra Group. Design structure modeled after Velde Equity (veldeequity.com), but with messaging that reflects Nostra's authentic positioning as an operator-buyer for founders of essential businesses who care about legacy.

---

## Brand Identity

**Company Name:** NOSTRA GROUP

**Location:** Based in the United States

**Tagline:** Operational Partnership for Essential Businesses

**Voice & Tone:** Direct, no-bullshit, operator-to-founder. The way you'd actually talk over coffee — not the way a PE marketing deck reads. Avoid corporate buzzwords, jargon, and empty phrases.
