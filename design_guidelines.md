# Nostra Group Website - Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference:** Velde Equity (veldeequity.com) - sophisticated professional investment firm aesthetic
**Tone:** Clean, professional, operator-focused. Direct and authentic, avoiding corporate jargon.

## Color System

**Backgrounds:**
- Primary: Bone White `#FAF9F6`
- Cards/Sections: Warm White `#FFFEF9`
- Alternate Sections: Ivory Accent `#F5F5F0`
- Dark Sections (Contact/Footer): `#0F172A`

**Text:**
- H1/H2: Deep Navy `#0A1628`
- H3/H4: Charcoal `#1C2A3A`
- Body: Slate `#374151`
- Secondary: Muted Gray `#6B7280`
- On Dark: `#F8FAFC`

**Accents:**
- Primary: Military Navy `#1E3A5F`
- Hover: Steel Blue `#2C5282`

## Typography
**Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- H1: 48px/3rem, weight 700
- H2: 36px/2.25rem, weight 600
- H3: 24px/1.5rem, weight 600
- H4: 20px/1.25rem, weight 600
- Body: 16px/1rem, weight 400
- Body Large: 18px/1.125rem, weight 400
- Caption: 14px/0.875rem, weight 400

## Layout System
**Spacing:** Use Tailwind units: 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 for consistent rhythm
**Containers:** max-w-7xl for main content, max-w-4xl for text-focused sections
**Section Padding:** py-16 to py-24 on desktop, py-12 on mobile

## Header/Navigation
- Sticky header with subtle backdrop blur
- Logo: "NOSTRA GROUP" left-aligned, bold
- Nav items: Home | About | Investment Criteria | Value Creation | Advisory | Contact
- Bone white background with shadow on scroll

## Page Specifications

### Home Page

**Hero Section:**
- Background: Bone White
- No hero image - clean, text-focused
- Main headline: "Operational Partnership for Essential Businesses"
- Company name prominently displayed
- Substantial subheadline explaining operator-buyer positioning
- Single CTA button

**What We Do (3-column cards):**
- Grid layout: 3 columns desktop, 1 column mobile
- Each card: title + multi-paragraph content
- Warm White card backgrounds
- Topics: Founder Transitions, Growth Partnerships, Essential Industries

**Why Nostra:**
- Ivory Accent background
- 3-pillar layout (can be stacked or side-by-side)
- Each pillar: bold title + detailed paragraph
- Bottom CTA: "GET IN TOUCH" button

### About Page
- Story section: single-column, max-w-4xl, generous line-height
- "What Makes Us Different": 5 cards in grid (2-col desktop, 1-col mobile)
- "How We Work": single-column narrative format

### Investment Criteria Page
- Focus cards: 5 items in grid layout
- Target Parameters: 4 stat cards displaying metrics
- "What We're Not" section: bulleted list, max-w-3xl

### Value Creation Page
- Framework: 5 pillars, each with title, description, and bulleted sub-items
- Technology Integration: detailed list with implementation focus

## Component Patterns

**Cards:**
- Warm White backgrounds
- Subtle borders or shadows
- Padding: p-8
- Rounded corners: rounded-lg

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Military Navy background
- Hover: Steel Blue
- Padding: px-8 py-3
- Rounded: rounded-md
- Font weight: 600

**Sections:**
- Alternate between Bone White and Ivory Accent backgrounds
- Consistent vertical spacing
- Clear visual hierarchy

## Images
**No hero image required** - this is a text-driven, professional site focused on substance over visual flair. Clean, typography-focused hero section.

**Minimal imagery throughout** - let the direct, operator-to-founder messaging take center stage.

## Critical Principles
- Professional, sophisticated, understated elegance
- Generous whitespace and breathing room
- Clear information hierarchy
- Direct, no-nonsense presentation matching brand voice
- Mobile-responsive throughout
- Accessibility: proper heading structure, sufficient color contrast
